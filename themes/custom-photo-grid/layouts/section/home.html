EngSoc Post Secret Service

The original Post-Secret project started in 2005, where people mailed in anonymous postcards with an unspoken and honest secret as contributions to an ongoing art project. While the original project operated as a continuously updating art exhibit both on and offline, numerous other post-secret movements have sprung up, including one right at Waterloo Engineering.

With the passing of Professor Igor Ivkovic, a beloved professor from the Systems Engineering Department, and former Dean Pearl Sullivan, who enthusiastically held the position for 7 years, we want to offer this space to students to share their thoughts, stories, struggles, and grief. Post-Secret wants to provide a channel for students to grieve the losses together. By posting in a public location, this term on our virtual wall, students can read and share in the knowledge that others have similar thoughts and that they really are not alone.

Content Warning
The posts on our site deal with sensitive topics so please read and share accordingly.

We encourage you to take a moment to reflect on your mental health and your experiences. If you have secrets, confessions, or just about anything you want to get off your chest, you can anonymously submit them here: bit.ly/PostSecretSubmitF20
